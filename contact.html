Nice to meet you, I'm Manuel

I am currently a full time student at PennLPS Coding bootcamp.

I have been in the graphic arts space for over 30 years. Born in New York City, I was raised in the south Bronx, and always admired the grafitti movement for it's sheer audacity. As the graf culture matured, the pieces evolved in it's complexity and structure. I fell in love with art, and combined my love for art with technology. I moved to the Spring Garden area of Philadelpha in the early '80's. I studied art at the Thomas Eakins House.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio.
